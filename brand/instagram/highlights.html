How to export these for Instagram

Open each highlight in Chrome DevTools at 1080 × 1920px. The icon and text are centered at the exact position Instagram will crop to its circle. Capture a full-size screenshot, then upload in Instagram → Profile → New Highlight → Edit Cover. The circle crop will naturally frame the centered icon. You can also build these in Canva at 1080×1920 with the icon centered at x=540 y=960.
