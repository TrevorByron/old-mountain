Our belief

There is a difference between taking a trip and having an experience that stays with you for life. If you've spent years dreaming about Africa, you already know which one you're after.

We believe the best guides for that experience aren't just experts in Africa — they're people who grew up calling it home.

Your guides along the journey.

Rooted There.
Fluent Here.

Jesse and Trevor Borden grew up in East Africa, went to school in the West, and have spent their lives moving comfortably between both. They understand what it means to dream about Africa from a distance — and they understand Africa itself in a way that only comes from growing up inside it. That combination is rare. It's the whole reason Old Mountain exists.

Jesse is a conservation ecologist with a PhD and over a decade of leading expeditions across Tanzania, Central America, and beyond. Trevor has been organizing and guiding people in East Africa since he was 19 years old. Together, they bring the kind of knowledge you can't find in a brochure — and the kind of care that makes you feel like you're traveling with family, not a vendor.

When you travel with Old Mountain, you're not handing your trip to a company. You're handing it to two people who have wanted to share this place their entire lives — and who will make sure you come home knowing you did it right.

Tanzania Beautiful

From the ancient traditions of the Hadzabe to the vast Serengeti plains, from the Ngorongoro Crater to the Ndutu wetlands — this journey is designed around what Old Mountain believes is the very best Tanzania has to offer. Built by people who call Africa home, it's an epic, deeply considered experience you can feel confident in.

The
Journey.
Eleven days.

This 11-day journey is operated in direct partnership with our longtime friends at Dorobo Safaris — arguably the finest safari operators in the region. We're working with Dorobo because their ethos resonates deeply with Old Mountain: unmatched field experience, deep local knowledge, and a steadfast commitment to caring for both the environment and the people who call it home.

Pricing.

One itinerary, two straightforward ways to book. Reserve your spot at our standard flat rate, or book as a pair and receive our couple rate. Pricing is per person and all-inclusive for the full 11-day expedition.

  • Standard rate: $10,900 per person
  • Couple rate: $10,100 per person (when booking two travelers together)
  • Dorobo walking guides and safari vehicles
  • All applicable park fees, taxes, and listed activities
  • All meals, plus snacks and drinking water in the vehicles
  • Local beers, select wines, soft drinks, juices, teas, coffee, and drinking water at Dorobo Mobile Camp, Lionspaw Lodge, and Esirai Migration Camp
  • Accommodation at hand-selected lodges and camps
  • Personal guidance from Jesse and Trevor throughout

What's not included

  • Tips and gratuities
  • Personal spending (for example: laundry, curios, and incidentals at lodges/camps)
  • Drinks at Tumbili Lodge (available for purchase)
