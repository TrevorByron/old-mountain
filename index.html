Meet

Your guides

Organizing and guiding since 2009

Jesse and Trevor grew up in the mountains of East Africa, spent their high school years on Portugal's Atlantic coast, and eventually found their way to California for college. Along the way, they built deep ties to people and places around the world and learned to move comfortably between the cultures and countries they have called home. They know what it means to chase adventure, and they design trips that blend outrageous fun, wild new places, and always, well made mixed drinks.

Jesse is a conservation ecologist with a PhD in interdisciplinary ecology and more than a decade of experience leading expeditions across East Africa, Central America, and the Arabian Peninsula. Trevor has been organizing trips and guiding groups in East Africa and Portugal since he was 19. After bikepacking across Europe and Mexico, backpacking through the Rift Valley, and motorcycling across much of East Africa, he is an expert at building adventures that are both distinctive and seamless. Together, they have traveled across six continents and lived long term on three, bringing the kind of insight you cannot find in a brochure and the kind of care that makes it feel like traveling with family.

When you travel with Old Mountain, you are not handing your trip to a company; you are joining an adventure with expert guides who are excited to share places and experiences that have shaped their lives. They will make sure you have what you need through every step and come home knowing you did it right.

The
Journey.
Eleven days.

This journey reflects what we love most about Africa: being out in it up close, on the ground, under the stars, and falling asleep to the sounds beyond the tent. It also celebrates Africa's beauty from extraordinary, deeply comfortable places.

We'll be partnering with Dorobo Safaris, our primary on the ground operator, because they are rooted in this landscape and lead with a rare balance of expertise, respect for local communities, and genuinely soulful guiding. Together, we've curated the full spectrum - from tented walking experiences and community connection in the Manyara ecosystem to epic lodges, vast vistas, and unforgettable sundowners - so guests feel the true breadth of Africa.

Specific itinerary details are subject to change based on availability, travel dates, and group size.

Day 01

Arrival into Arusha

International arrival, transfer and settle in. Your African adventure begins tonight.

Day 02

Half-day Arusha, afternoon to the Manyara Ecosystem

Morning in town, then head north into the rift highlands to the Manyara Ecosystem for a more old-Africa rhythm in the field.

Day 03

Full day - Manyara Ecosystem experiences

Walking, wildlife tracking, and time with local communities, including meaningful time with the Maasai community, in a less curated, on-the-ground experience away from the air-conditioned windows.

Day 04

Drive to the Yaeda Valley — Hadzabe activities

Cross into the remote Yaeda Valley and spend the afternoon with the Hadzabe, one of the last hunter-gatherer peoples on earth.

Day 05

Full day with the Hadzabe

A rare and extraordinary day living alongside and learning from the Hadzabe community.

Day 06

Morning with the Hadzabe — drive to Ngorongoro

A final morning in the Yaeda Valley before transferring to the Ngorongoro highlands.

Day 07

Full day — Ngorongoro Crater, game drives

Descend into the world's largest intact volcanic caldera, a natural amphitheater teeming with wildlife including the Big Five.

Day 08

Full day — Ndutu area and plains, game drives

Enter the Ndutu wetlands, a calving ground for wildebeest and one of the most ecologically rich corners of the Serengeti ecosystem.

Day 09

Central and Eastern Serengeti — game drives

Venture into the heart of the Serengeti. Vast skies, infinite plains, and the great migration on the move.

Day 10

Ndutu or Serengeti — game drives

A final full day in the wilderness. Follow the wildlife, follow your instincts, follow the light.

Day 11

Fly to Arusha — international departure

Morning flight back to Arusha. Farewell lunch at Tumbili Lodge before your international connection home.
